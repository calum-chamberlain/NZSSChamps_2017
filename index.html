New Zealand Single-Speed Champs 2017
Supported by ParrotDog.

Mt Victoria, Wellington
Sunday 23 April 2017

Presented by Hüttcross

For the first time, the New Zealand Single Speed Championships descends upon Wellington NZ. Make no mistake, it is a serious race, in a very silly manner of speaking. Costumes are encouraged so firmly as to be basically mandatory - lycra will only be permitted ironically.

The rules are simple. Your bike may only have one speed. We don’t care if you ride a genuine single speed, a BMX, or a mountain bike with the shifters duct-taped in place. The second most important rule is that the winners get tattoos. We wish to be clear, if you do not want a tattoo, do not win the race.

This humble edition is sponsored by local brewery ParrotDog. They are lovely people, they make a superb brew. Please support them - give them a like or a follow, drink lots of their beer.

We have some tasty surprises in store, attendees shall not walk away disappointed.

See y’all Anzac Weekend.
